# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the
[Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/)
verbatim. The full text is available at the link above and is also
mirrored across the GitHub community in
[contributor-covenant/contributor_covenant](https://github.com/contributor-covenant/contributor_covenant).

## Summary

We pledge to make participation in this community a harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity. We are
committed to acting and interacting in ways that contribute to an open,
welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

Examples of expected behaviour: empathy, respect for differing
viewpoints, graceful acceptance of constructive feedback, accountability
for mistakes, and focus on what's best for the community.

Examples of unacceptable behaviour: harassment in any form, trolling,
insulting or derogatory comments, public or private harassment,
publishing others' private information without permission, and any
conduct reasonably considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces (issues, pull
requests, discussions, the codebase itself) and when an individual is
officially representing the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may
be reported privately to the maintainers at **ishrathahamed0@gmail.com**.
All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly,
and the reporter's privacy will be respected.

Maintainers who do not follow or enforce the Code of Conduct in good
faith may face temporary or permanent repercussions as determined by
other members of the project's leadership.
